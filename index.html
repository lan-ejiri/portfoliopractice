About Me

My name is Lan Ejiri and I am a fullstack web developer with certification from the Rutgers Web Development Coding Bootcamp. I also have a Bachelors Degree in Industrial Engineering.


Right after graduating, I picked up a job as a Draftsman at a small woodwoorking tool manufacturing company. I gained many valuable work experiences from working there for a year and a half, such at learning how to get along with coworkers and getting used to demanding work flow. However, this was not what I wanted to do for the rest of my life. I hoped that with my background in engineering, learning to code would help me land a better job.


My experiences before this drafting job includes mostly different types of tutoring. I have done 1-on-1 sessions with college peers for classes I took in previous semesters. I have worked for giant corporations such as C2 education in a 3-on-1 setting helping high schoolers get stellar SAT/ACT math scores to get into their dream college. I have done private tutoring for Japanese families to help their children be better adjusted for when they start school in the fall. These experiences really forced me to learn how to handle many different types of people, and I believe I am a more well-rounded person because of these opportunities.


I would like to earn a job in the tech field for a large company, but I am happy to pick up any other jobs to gain experience and become a better coder.
